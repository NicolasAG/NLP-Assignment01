US election facts that may surprise. Turnout was not low, opinion polls were not far out and Clinton's popular vote lead is growing. The US presidential election has given rise to a wide range of responses and opinions. But some aspects of it are rooted in fact - not open to interpretation. Here, we present a compendium of sorts about the election. Some of these may seem basic, but they are by no means obvious to everyone. Hillary Clinton won the popular vote, and that lead is only going to grow. The New York Times called the election for Donald Trump at about 2.40am eastern time on November 9th.
